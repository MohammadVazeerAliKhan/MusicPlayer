MC Stan Wiki Biography-One of the newest hip-hop and rap musicians from Pune, Maharashtra, India, goes by the stage name MC Stan or his real name Altaf Tadavi or Altaf Shaikh. He was one of the most contentious rappers in the Indian rap music scene and the Desi hip-hop culture when he was born on August 30, 1999. In 2018, he dropped his debut single, “Wata,” and ever since then, he's been on a trailblazing quest to keep dropping hits. He has a number of songs to his credit, and his most recent single, "Shana Bann," is dominating the Indian rap music charts. One of India's most talented and youthful rappers is Altaf Tadavi, better known by his stage name MC Stan.
